Constellation Directory


First Mission; about

Welcome to Constellation Fest 2023, the ultimate celebration of sound! Prepare to be blown away by a breathtaking fusion of genres and electrifying performances that will leave you spellbound. This year, we've curated an unrivaled lineup, bringing together music's brightest stars from across the globe. Feel the pulsating beats of world-renowned DJs, lose yourself in the mesmerizing melodies of indie sensations, and get ready to rock out with legendary bands. Immerse yourself in a vibrant atmosphere, adorned with captivating visuals and mind-bending installations. Indulge in mouthwatering culinary delights and sip on signature cocktails as you dance the night away. Constellation Fest 2023 will redefine your festival experience and leave you craving more.


Second Mission; our talent

At Constellation Fest 2023, prepare to witness an extraordinary lineup of talent that will ignite your senses and elevate your musical journey. Get ready to sway to the soulful tunes of Grammy-winning artists, as their powerful voices captivate your heart. Discover the enchanting sounds of emerging artists, ready to make their mark on the industry. Brace yourself for mind-blowing instrumental virtuosity as renowned musicians unleash their mastery on stage. Experience the energy of explosive hip-hop performances that will have you bouncing to the rhythm. And don't forget the electrifying collaborations that will create once-in-a-lifetime moments you'll cherish forever. Constellation Fest 2023 is a melting pot of musical brilliance, promising unforgettable memories for all who attend.


Final Mission; the world

After the resounding success of Constellation Fest 2019, the festival is embarking on an unprecedented world tour across South America and Europe, spreading its magical vibes to music lovers across continents. Get ready to witness the fusion of diverse cultures and musical traditions as artists from different corners of the globe unite on a single stage. From the vibrant streets of Rio de Janeiro to the historic arenas of Rome, the festival will transform iconic locations into immersive sonic wonderlands. Experience the rhythmic beats of samba in Brazil, the soul-stirring flamenco in Spain, and the pulsating electronic scene in Germany. The Constellation Fest world tour promises to be an exhilarating journey, celebrating the universal language of music.
